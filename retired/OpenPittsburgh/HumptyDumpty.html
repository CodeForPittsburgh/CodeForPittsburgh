   The Humpty Dumpty Plan

 

Humpty Dumpty sat on a wall,
Humpty Dumpty had a great fall.
All the king's horses and all the king's men
Couldn't put Humpty together again

Over the past year, CodeForPittsburgh has been gathering data from the City of Pittsburgh Police Weekly Blotter. Their data lasts for only one week; although the Western Regional Data Center now has a historical collection.

The CodeForPittsburgh Police Blotter Team has developed a number of map, table and data views for the community use.

We have added latitude, longitude, council district and zip code that is not part of the City data file.

We are thinking that it might be useful for the community to get Police Incident data that they could create their own specific visual displays.

So, why Humpty Dumpty? The data CodeForPittsburgh has stored in it's databases are not accessible except through the views we have provided.

Coming soon, you will be able to download csv file data based on selection styles currently in the Heat Map.

The csv file type allows you to look at the data in Microsoft Excel.
